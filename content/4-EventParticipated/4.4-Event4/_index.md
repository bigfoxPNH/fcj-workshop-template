# SUMMARY REPORT: EVENT 4

### Event Objectives

To create a knowledge-sharing space on technology, providing real-world experiences and perspectives from the enterprise environment, while guiding career mindset and readiness for the tech community in the era of AI transformation.

### Key Highlights

#### Sharing Career Path & Supporting Tech Professionals in Adapting to AI
Provide long-term career direction for students and young engineers; share how to develop AI Agent platforms to support and amplify human capabilities in operating complex and critical Cloud Infrastructure.

#### Building and Optimizing AI Voice Solutions for the Vietnamese Market
Promote research and practice of Voice AI solutions in Vietnamese; guide Speech-to-Text conversion techniques combined with LLM for smooth context processing and intelligent handover mechanisms from AI to humans in complex enterprise scenarios.

#### Proactive Detection and Automated Incident Response
Introduce DevOps AI Agent solutions that automate log extraction, build topology diagrams, and propose mitigation plans whenever system incidents occur, thereby minimizing Mean Time to Recovery (MTTR) for businesses.

#### Modernizing HR Management Strategy with AI Technology
Optimize HR department performance through technology automation of repetitive tasks such as CV screening, candidate-JD matching; eliminate subjective bias and ensure absolute security of internal company data.

#### Establishing In-depth Enterprise Security Architecture
Address enterprise-grade infrastructure security challenges; guide setup of private security connections via VPC Connection to connect AI Agents with third-party servers (MCP Server) without exposing information through the public Internet.

### Key Takeaways

* How to build a sustainable career path in the AI era and the importance of continuous skill updating.
* Voice AI deployment techniques for Vietnamese: Speech-to-Text combined with LLM and intelligent handover mechanisms.
* Automated incident detection and response process with DevOps AI Agent, reducing system recovery time (MTTR).
* AI applications in HR management: automated CV screening, candidate matching, and internal data security.
* Enterprise security architecture: setting up secure VPC Connection for AI Agents to connect with MCP Servers.
