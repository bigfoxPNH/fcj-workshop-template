# SUMMARY REPORT: EVENT 3

### Event Objectives

* Organize an academic AWS playground (AWS Certification Quiz) for intern students.
* Test and reinforce AWS service knowledge through a team-based competition format.

### Key Highlights

* Exchange and learn AWS knowledge through fun, engaging questions.

### Key Takeaways

* The judges provided detailed explanations of why each answer was correct or incorrect.
* This deepened my understanding of AWS services.

### Event Experience

Participating in the **AWS Certification Quiz** was an exciting and thrilling experience, allowing me to review my AWS knowledge while competing alongside fellow interns. Some standout experiences:

#### Vibrant competition atmosphere
* The event gathered **8 teams** determined to conquer AWS questions across **10 different question sets**.
* The intense competitive spirit among teams created strong motivation for every member to give their best.

#### Reinforced AWS knowledge
* Questions spanned across multiple AWS services, helping me systematize my existing knowledge.
* The judges' detailed explanations of correct/incorrect answers deepened my understanding of each service.

#### Learning through competition
* The team quiz format made learning more engaging and memorable compared to passive studying.
* Real-world question scenarios helped me understand how to apply AWS services to solve specific problems.

#### Connecting with peers
* This was a great opportunity to network and learn among fellow interns.
* Team spirit flourished as members discussed and agreed on answers together.

#### Lessons learned
* AWS knowledge is vast — it requires regular review combined with hands-on practice to master.
* Learning through competition and Q&A improves memory retention and quick reflexes.
* Better preparation is needed for future quizzes to achieve better results.
